About EduLearn

EduLearn is an initiative to provide high-quality educational content to children in a simple, accessible, and fun way. We believe that education should be engaging and available to every child, regardless of location or background.

Our mission is to empower students and parents with free and affordable learning resources. From well-designed notes to interactive teaching videos, we aim to make learning joyful and effective for kids from Class 1 to Class 5.

We are a passionate team of educators, developers, and content creators working together to build a platform that truly helps children grow in their learning journey.
